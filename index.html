A Journey That Changed the Way I See the World: Travel is not just about visiting new places; it’s about discovering parts of yourself you never knew existed. This realization truly hit me during my recent trip, a journey that began as a simple escape from routine but turned into an unforgettable life experience. The moment I arrived, I felt a strange sense of calm. The air was different—lighter, fresher—and everything seemed to move at its own pace. There was no rush, no pressure, just people living in the moment. As I walked through unfamiliar streets, I noticed small details I usually ignore in daily life: the sound of footsteps on old roads, the smell of local food, and the warmth of strangers who smiled without any reason.
